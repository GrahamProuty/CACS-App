📚 Free Resources from CACS

Capital Area Community Services offers these programs at no cost to you.

💬

Free Financial Counseling

One-on-one sessions with a trained counselor to help you create a budget, manage debt, and plan for the future. Confidential and judgment-free.

📖

"Your Money, Your Goals" Toolkit

A free workbook from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) with step-by-step tools for managing money, setting goals, and dealing with debt. Ask your CACS counselor for a copy.

🏠

Property Tax Assistance

Help understanding and applying for property tax credits and exemptions that could lower your housing costs. Available to qualifying homeowners in the service area.

📄

Free Tax Preparation (VITA)

IRS-certified volunteers prepare your federal and state tax returns for free. Available during tax season for households earning under $67,000/year. Don't pay for something you can get free!
